How to use this invoice generator

  1. Fill in your details — your business name, your client, invoice number and dates.
  2. Add line items — a description, quantity and rate for each service or product. The totals update live.
  3. Download the PDF — click “Download PDF” and choose Save as PDF in the print dialog. Send it to your client by email.

Frequently asked questions

Is this invoice generator really free?

Yes — completely free, with no watermark, no account, and no limit on how many invoices you create.

Where is my data stored?

Only in your own browser (localStorage). Nothing you type is sent to any server, which also makes this tool safe for confidential client work.

What should an invoice include?

A unique invoice number, your business name and contact details, the client's details, an issue date and due date, a clear description of each item, the amounts, any tax, and how you'd like to be paid.

Can I use it for my profession?

Yes. If you want a head start, we have pre-filled invoice examples for 20+ professions — designers, developers, photographers, cleaners, consultants and more.
