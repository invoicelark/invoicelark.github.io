Frequently asked questions

Should I show the client this breakdown?

Show the deliverables and the price, not the internal math. Itemize by outcome (“Homepage design — 2 concepts, 2 revision rounds”), never by hour, or you invite line-item haggling.

The final number feels high. Should I lower it?

Lower the scope, not the price. Offer a smaller package if budget is tight. Cutting the price for identical work just tells the client the first number was padding.

How should I invoice a project like this?

A deposit (30–50%) before you start, the balance on delivery. Generate both invoices with our free invoice generator and put the payment terms right on them.
