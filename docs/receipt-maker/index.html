Receipt vs. invoice — which one do you need?

An invoice is a request for payment — you send it before the money arrives. A receipt is proof of payment — you issue it after. If you still need to get paid, use our free invoice generator first, then come back here to issue the receipt once the payment lands.

Frequently asked questions

Is this receipt maker free?

Completely free — no watermark, no account, no limits.

Is a printed receipt from this tool valid?

Yes. A receipt is valid when it identifies the seller, the payer, the date, the amount and what it was for — all of which this tool includes. Some jurisdictions require tax details for business receipts; add yours in the business field if needed.

Where is my data stored?

Only in your browser's local storage on your own device. Nothing is uploaded anywhere.

Can I use it for cash payments?

That's its most common use — cash payments leave no paper trail of their own, so a written receipt protects both sides.
