Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 2026

The short version

Everything you type into InvoiceLark tools stays on your device. We have no servers that receive, store, or process your invoice or calculator data.

Data you enter into tools

The invoice generator and calculators run entirely in your browser using JavaScript. Data you enter (client names, amounts, notes) may be saved to your browser's local storage on your own device so your work isn't lost between visits. You can erase it anytime with each tool's "Clear" button or by clearing your browser storage. This data is never transmitted to us or to any third party by our code.

Analytics and advertising

The site may use privacy-respecting analytics to count visits, and may display advertising (such as Google AdSense) or affiliate links in the future. Ad providers may use cookies as described in their own policies; where required, you will be asked for consent. Affiliate links are clearly marked and cost you nothing.

Hosting

The site is served as static files by our hosting provider, which may log standard technical data (IP address, user agent) for security and operational purposes, as described in the provider's own privacy policy.
