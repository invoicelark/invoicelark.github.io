About InvoiceLark

Free, no-nonsense tools for the money side of freelancing.

InvoiceLark exists because the business side of freelancing — invoicing, pricing, chasing payments — is the part nobody signs up for. The tools here are built to get those jobs done in minutes, without creating an account, installing anything, or handing your client data to yet another service.

Privacy by design: every tool on this site runs entirely in your browser. What you type into an invoice or calculator is stored only on your own device (in your browser's local storage) and is never transmitted to us — we couldn't read it if we wanted to.

How the site sustains itself: the site may display advertising or recommend services we believe are genuinely useful to freelancers. Recommendations are always clearly marked and never change how the tools work or what they cost (nothing — they're free).
